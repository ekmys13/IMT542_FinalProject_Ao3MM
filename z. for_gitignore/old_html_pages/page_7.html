    “So what do you need, Sean?” She looked at him intently, hoping he could hear the possibility in her voice. She didn’t want to scare him off, or make any assumptions. He’d have to ask. (The asking, though, the nervousness of it all, always excited Jinnah.)

    “What do I need? Great fucking question, dr. Jean.” He ran his hands through his hair, took a deep breath in.

    “Where’s Marion?” he asked rather quickly.

    Jean chuckled. ‘Subtle,’ she thought.
